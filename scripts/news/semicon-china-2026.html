NineScrolls Partner Nano-Promiso Showcases Compound Semiconductor Solutions at SEMICON China 2026

Beijing Nano-Promiso Technology Co., Ltd., a strategic partner of NineScrolls LLC, exhibited at SEMICON China 2026 in Shanghai this March. At Booth E7366, the team showcased its compound semiconductor product portfolio — spanning GaN and GaAs substrates, process equipment, scrubber systems, and gas delivery solutions — alongside NineScrolls' plasma etching and thin film deposition capabilities.

Event Highlights

SEMICON China is the world's premier semiconductor industry event, drawing thousands of professionals from across the global supply chain. This year's edition, held under the theme "Transform Tomorrow," featured exhibitors spanning materials, equipment, and services for both silicon and compound semiconductor manufacturing.

Nano-Promiso's Booth E7366 featured detailed technical displays covering the company's four core product lines, drawing interest from researchers, fab engineers, and procurement teams working in power electronics, RF, and optoelectronics.

What Was Showcased

GaN & GaAs Substrates

HVPE-grown GaN freestanding wafers and GaAs substrates for power electronics, RF, and optoelectronic applications. These products feature industry-leading crystal quality, TTV control, and surface roughness specifications — essential for high-performance device fabrication.

Process Equipment

A comprehensive range of semiconductor fabrication tools including MOCVD, plasma treatment, coating, dicing, and bonding systems. These support the full epitaxial wafer manufacturing workflow from substrate preparation through device processing.

Scrubber Systems (SEMI S2 Certified)

Exhaust gas treatment systems designed for compound semiconductor and silicon process chemistries. All scrubber systems carry SEMI S2 certification, meeting international safety and environmental compliance standards — a key requirement for fabs seeking global market access.

Gas Cabinet Systems

Safe handling and delivery solutions for process gases used in semiconductor manufacturing, engineered for reliability, safety, and seamless integration with existing fab infrastructure.

Combined Portfolio: Nano-Promiso × NineScrolls

Through its partnership with NineScrolls LLC, Nano-Promiso extends its reach to international markets. NineScrolls specializes in research-grade plasma etching (RIE/ICP), atomic layer deposition (ALD), and chemical vapor deposition (CVD/PECVD/HDP-CVD) equipment — serving universities, research institutions, and corporate R&D labs worldwide.

Together, the two companies provide a single point of contact for substrates, deposition, etch, process tools, and environmental safety systems — with localized technical support for international customers.
